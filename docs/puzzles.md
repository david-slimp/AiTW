# Puzzle Patterns (Examples + Ideas)

Purpose: collect generic puzzle types and examples to adapt for AiTW (avoid copy-paste of other games, focus on styles).

## Progression Gates
- Item gate: required item unlocks a door or path.
- Knowledge gate: learned phrase/verse unlocks a response or path.
- Sequence gate: actions in a correct order open a new area.

## Environment Interaction
- Light/dark: visibility changes based on light sources.
- Sound/silence: noise triggers consequences or reveals hints.
- Timed event: a door opens only after a trigger elsewhere.

## Social/Relational
- Dialogue gate: correct response or empathy unlocks help.
- Trust gate: complete a service to gain access or info.

## Inventory/Combination
- Combine items: two items create a usable tool.
- Substitute items: multiple possible solutions (gentle vs direct).

## Observation/Inference
- Pattern recognition: symbols or repeated motifs indicate a path.
- Recontextualization: revisit a place with new understanding.

## Spiritual/Thematic
- Repentance check: a wrong choice requires repentance to proceed.
- Fruit gate: demonstrate patience/faithfulness to unlock a path.

## Language and Cryptic Puzzles
- Translation/Ciphers: decode a lost or symbolic language using a key or contextual clues.
- Riddles: an NPC or inscription poses a riddle requiring insight or lateral thinking.
- Wordplay: anagrams or “missing word” puzzles based on phrases or verses.

## Physical and Spatial Puzzles
- Transformation/State Change: alter an item’s state to make it usable (purify, freeze, cleanse).
- Weight/Balance: place items with specific weight to open or hold a path.
- Navigation Mazes: classic mazes or non‑Euclidean routes that require careful mapping.
- Symmetry/Alignment: arrange objects to match a discovered pattern or orientation.

## Logic and System Puzzles
- Resource Management: limited resources force a righteous or efficient choice.
- Truth‑Teller/Liar: find the true path by asking a correct, constrained question.
- Process/Machine Mastery: operate a complex system via found instructions or trial and error.

## Meta and Narrative Puzzles
- Perspective Shift: change viewpoint (e.g., spiritual sight) to reveal hidden elements.
- Metapuzzles: several smaller puzzles unlock a larger revelation.
- Character Transformation: progress only after a narrative change (title, new name, calling).

## Teamwork/Cooperation Puzzles
- Simultaneous Action: coordinate with an NPC in a different location to trigger a result.
- Sacrifice/Altruism: give up a valued item to help an NPC who later helps you.

## Notes for AiTW
- Prefer puzzles that teach or embody discipleship themes.
- Keep early puzzles short and fair; provide clear clues.
